About me

I lead design teams.
I still open Figma every day.

With 16+ years spanning B2B SaaS, Automotive, and Telecom, I sit at the intersection of design strategy and hands-on craft. I build design chapters, establish DesignOps processes, and set the technical direction for how a design team works — not just what they produce.

At Valeo, I introduced Figma Make for AI-driven component generation and NotebookLM for research synthesis — got team-wide adoption, and watched delivery cycles compress noticeably. I vibe code interactive stakeholder demos that compress weeks of back-and-forth into one session.

I operate with a design-to-cost mindset: every decision is weighed against engineering reality from day one, not at handoff. This is how you build systems developers love to implement.
